About Us

R&C Utilities Limited is your trusted partner for infrastructure services across the UK.

With over 15 years of combined experience in the utilities and telecoms industry, our leadership team brings unmatched expertise and practical knowledge to every project.

Founded by an experienced contracts manager and a long-term industry trainer, we offer a full suite of civils and telecoms services designed around safety, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

We are telecoms specialists in all aspects of fibre and copper cabling, including blowing, splicing, and testing. Whether working on new installations, upgrades, or fault resolution, our engineers bring precision and professionalism to every task.

We also have a reputation for delivering high-quality finishes for residential driveways, commercial car parks, and reinstatement works. Our team ensures every surface is built to last, fully compliant with regulations and completed to the highest standard.
